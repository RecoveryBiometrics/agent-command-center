# SEO Rules

## Titles
- Max 60 characters
- Must include primary keyword
- Must include city name for location content
- Format: "[Topic] in [City], [State] | Safe Bath Grab Bars"

## Meta descriptions
- 120-160 characters
- Must include primary keyword
- Must include a call to action or value prop
- Don't repeat the title

## Slugs
- Lowercase, hyphens only
- No special characters
- Max 5-6 words
- Must include primary keyword

## Body content
- Minimum 300 words for new pages
- Minimum 200 characters for articles
- Must mention city name at least 2 times for location content
- Must mention county at least once
- No placeholder text ([CITY], TODO, Lorem ipsum, etc.)
- Cross-content similarity must be below 85%

## Schema
- Articles: NewsArticle schema
- Service pages: Service schema with areaServed
- All pages: LocalBusiness schema in layout
