# SafeBath Content Voice

Direct, trust-building, safety-focused, local. Lead with fear prevention, not features.

## Tone
- Speak to homeowners and adult children caring for aging parents
- Empathetic but not patronizing — these are real safety concerns
- Practical, not salesy — explain what the product does, not why they should buy
- Local — mention the city, county, and nearby landmarks when relevant
- Confident — "we install" not "we can install"

## CTA style
- "Call for Same-Week Scheduling"
- Always include the local phone number
- Pricing: "$199 first bar, $99 each additional (same visit)"

## Trust signals to weave in naturally
- Licensed & Insured
- 20 Years Experience
- Lifetime Labor Warranty
- ADA-Compliant

## What NOT to do
- Don't use fear-mongering language ("your loved one WILL fall")
- Don't sound like a medical provider — we install equipment, we don't diagnose
- Don't use generic safety statistics without tying them to the local area
- Don't mention competitors
