# Relocation plan

## The plan

Bill is planning to relocate from Doylestown, PA to South Venice Beach, FL. The timing and exact date are not locked, but the intent is in place and aligned with the rest of the 12-month plan.

## Why

1. **Florida is the launch market for the ice machine business.** Per the ice-machines playbook, Florida is the year-one target market. Being in-market means easier site visits, faster permitting, zero travel cost for early installations, and real-time management of the first machines.

2. **No state income tax in Florida.** Pennsylvania state income tax is approximately 3.07% flat. At $100K+ income, moving the tax home to Florida saves meaningful money. The ice machine business, once profitable, amplifies this benefit.

3. **Florida LLC formation is already on the month-1 task list.** Living in-state simplifies the LLC formation, registered agent, and any future multi-entity structuring.

4. **Central Florida bass fishing infrastructure is 45-90 minutes from South Venice Beach.** Close enough for operations, far enough from tourist density to live normally.
