# Tier 3: Market-Specific Topic Generation

## What this tier covers
Topics that GHL docs DON'T cover — industry verticals × local markets.
Examples: "Why plumbers in Mexico need a CRM", "How Dubai real estate agents automate WhatsApp"

## Industry lists per market

### LATAM (Spanish)
- Real estate (inmobiliarias)
- Healthcare (consultorios, clínicas)
- Restaurants (restaurantes, cafeterías)
- Fitness (gimnasios, estudios)
- Education (escuelas, academias)
- Legal (abogados, despachos)
- Beauty (salones, estéticas)
- Auto dealerships (agencias de autos)
- E-commerce (tiendas en línea)
- Tourism (agencias de viajes, hoteles)

### India
- Real estate (agencies, builders)
- Healthcare (clinics, hospitals, telemedicine)
- Education / EdTech (coaching, online courses)
- Wedding planning
- CA firms (chartered accountants)
- Restaurants / food delivery
- Coaching / consulting
- E-commerce (D2C brands)
- Fitness / yoga studios
- Auto dealers

### MENA (Arabic)
- Real estate (Dubai, Riyadh, Cairo)
- Hospitality (hotels, resorts)
- Healthcare (clinics, dental, dermatology)
- Luxury retail
- Food delivery / restaurants
- Fitness / personal training
- Consulting / professional services
- E-commerce
- Events / wedding planning
- Education / tutoring

## Topic generation pattern
Each topic should combine:
1. A specific industry from the list above
2. A specific country or city
3. A GHL feature relevant to that industry
4. A pain point the business owner has

Example: "How Dubai real estate agents use GoHighLevel to automate WhatsApp follow-ups and close deals faster"

## Avoid
- Generic topics that could apply to any market
- Topics already covered by Tier 1 (GHL docs)
- Topics too niche to have search volume
